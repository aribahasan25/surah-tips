Quran - Surahs 31 to 40

31. Luqman

Meaning: Wisdom of Luqman; advice, guidance, and belief in Allah.

32. As-Sajda (The Prostration)

Meaning: Importance of faith, prostration, and worship of Allah.

33. Al-Ahzab (The Confederates)

Meaning: Battles, obedience, and social laws for believers.

34. Saba (Sheba)

Meaning: Story of Sheba; gratitude, guidance, and faith.

35. Fatir (The Originator)

Meaning: Allah as the creator; guidance, mercy, and accountability.

36. Ya-Sin

Meaning: Heart of the Quran; reminders of resurrection and guidance.

37. As-Saffat (Those Who Set The Ranks)

Meaning: Stories of prophets; accountability and signs of Allah.

38. Sad

Meaning: Stories of prophets and lessons for believers.

39. Az-Zumar (The Groups)

Meaning: Worship, faith, and the consequences of deeds.
