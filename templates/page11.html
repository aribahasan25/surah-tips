Quran - Surahs 101 to 110

101. Al-Qari‘ah (The Calamity)

Meaning: The Day of Judgment; people’s deeds weighed; fate of light and heavy scales.

102. At-Takathur (The Rivalry in Worldly Increase)

Meaning: Warning against worldly rivalry; accountability in the Hereafter.

103. Al-‘Asr (The Time)

Meaning: Mankind in loss except those with faith, good deeds, truth, and patience.

104. Al-Humazah (The Slanderer)

Meaning: Warning against slanderers and hoarders of wealth; punishment in Hell.

105. Al-Fil (The Elephant)

Meaning: Allah’s destruction of the army of the elephant that attacked the Ka‘bah.

106. Quraysh

Meaning: Allah’s blessings upon Quraysh; command to worship the Lord of the Ka‘bah.

107. Al-Ma‘un (The Small Kindnesses)

Meaning: Condemnation of those neglecting prayers and refusing small kindness.

108. Al-Kawthar (The Abundance)

Meaning: Allah’s gift of abundance to the Prophet (ﷺ); command to pray and sacrifice.

109. Al-Kafirun (The Disbelievers)

Meaning: Clear separation between believers and disbelievers in worship.

110. An-Nasr (The Divine Help)

Meaning: Prophecy of Islam’s victory; command to glorify and seek forgiveness.
