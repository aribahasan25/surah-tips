Quran - Surahs 51 to 60

51. Adh-Dhariyat

Meaning: Resurrection, destruction of past nations, and signs in creation.

52. At-Tur

Meaning: Punishment of disbelievers, rewards of believers in Paradise.

53. An-Najm

Meaning: The Prophet’s message is divine revelation, not personal opinion.

54. Al-Qamar

Meaning: Splitting of the moon, past prophets’ stories, warnings of judgment.

55. Ar-Rahman

Meaning: Allah’s countless favors, Paradise & Hell, repeated question to mankind & jinn.

56. Al-Waqi’ah

Meaning: Last Day, division of people into three groups: foremost, righteous, disbelievers.

57. Al-Hadid

Meaning: Charity, balance of life, iron as strength.

58. Al-Mujadila

Meaning: Justice, secret meetings, alliances.

59. Al-Hashr

Meaning: Expulsion of tribes, unity of believers, Allah’s beautiful names.

60. Al-Mumtahanah

Meaning: Rules for women migrating for Islam, guidance with non-Muslims.
