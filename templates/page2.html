Quran - Surahs 11 to 20

11. Hud

Meaning: Stories of Prophet Hud and lessons for guidance and patience.

12. Yusuf (Joseph)

Meaning: Story of Prophet Yusuf (Joseph); patience, faith, and trust in Allah.

13. Ar-Ra’d (The Thunder)

Meaning: Allah’s power, revelation, and guidance.

14. Ibrahim

Meaning: Stories of Prophet Ibrahim; gratitude, prayer, and guidance.

15. Al-Hijr

Meaning: Warning for disbelievers; stories of past nations.

16. An-Nahl (The Bee)

Meaning: Allah’s blessings, guidance, and accountability.

17. Al-Isra (The Night Journey)

Meaning: The Night Journey of the Prophet; laws, guidance, and worship.

18. Al-Kahf (The Cave)

Meaning: Story of the people of the cave; faith, patience, and trials.

19. Maryam (Mary)

Meaning: Story of Maryam and Prophet Isa (Jesus); miracles and guidance.

20. Ta-Ha

Meaning: Story of Prophet Musa (Moses); guidance, patience, and miracles.
