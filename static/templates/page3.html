Quran - Surahs 21 to 30

21. Al-Anbiya (The Prophets)

Meaning: Stories of prophets and the Day of Judgment.

22. Al-Hajj (The Pilgrimage)

Meaning: Pilgrimage rituals, trials of faith, and resurrection.

23. Al-Mu’minun (The Believers)

Meaning: Qualities of true believers and their reward.

24. An-Nur (The Light)

Meaning: Social laws, modesty, and Allah’s light.

25. Al-Furqan (The Criterion)

Meaning: Criterion between right and wrong, qualities of true servants.

26. Ash-Shu‘ara (The Poets)

Meaning: Stories of prophets; Quran is not poetry.

27. An-Naml (The Ant)

Meaning: Story of Prophet Solomon and the Queen of Sheba.

28. Al-Qasas (The Stories)

Meaning: Story of Moses and Pharaoh.

29. Al-Ankabut (The Spider)

Meaning: Weakness of false gods; tests of faith.

30. Ar-Rum (The Romans)

Meaning: Prophecy of Roman victory, signs of Allah.
