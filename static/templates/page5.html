Quran - Surahs 41 to 50

41. Fussilat (Explained in Detail)

Meaning: Signs of Allah in creation and warnings for disbelievers.

42. Ash-Shura (The Consultation)

Meaning: Importance of consultation, mercy, and guidance.

43. Az-Zukhruf (Gold Adornments)

Meaning: Worldly temptations vs. eternal faith.

44. Ad-Dukhan (The Smoke)

Meaning: Signs of Allah and warnings about judgment.

45. Al-Jathiya (The Crouching)

Meaning: All nations will kneel on the Day of Judgment.

46. Al-Ahqaf (The Wind-Curved Sandhills)

Meaning: Story of Aad, guidance, and warnings for deniers.

47. Muhammad

Meaning: About Prophet Muhammad ﷺ, jihad, and faith.

48. Al-Fath (The Victory)

Meaning: Treaty of Hudaybiyyah and promise of victory.

49. Al-Hujurat (The Rooms)

Meaning: Social manners, unity, and brotherhood in Islam.

50. Qaf

Meaning: Resurrection, judgment, and reminders of Allah’s power.
