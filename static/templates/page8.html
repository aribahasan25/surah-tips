Quran - Surahs 71 to 80

71. Nuh (Noah)

Meaning: Prophet Nuh’s message to his people, their rejection, and his prayer to Allah.

72. Al-Jinn (The Jinn)

Meaning: Describes how jinn listened to the Quran and their belief, warning humans and jinn alike.

73. Al-Muzzammil (The Enshrouded One)

Meaning: Urges the Prophet to pray at night, be patient, and trust Allah’s plan.

74. Al-Muddaththir (The Cloaked One)

Meaning: Calls Prophet Muhammad to rise and warn, avoid idols, and be steadfast.

75. Al-Qiyamah (The Resurrection)

Meaning: Vivid description of the Day of Judgment, fate of souls, and accountability.

76. Al-Insan (Man)

Meaning: Discusses creation of humans, trials, and rewards of the righteous in Paradise.

77. Al-Mursalat (The Emissaries)

Meaning: Oaths about the Day of Judgment, describing fate of deniers versus believers.

78. An-Naba (The Tidings)

Meaning: Talks about resurrection, cosmic signs, and final judgment between believers and disbelievers.

79. An-Nazi’at (Those Who Drag Forth)

Meaning: Angels removing souls, story of Pharaoh, and certainty of resurrection.

80. Abasa (He Frowned)

Meaning: Incident where Prophet frowned, reminder that Quran is for all – rich or poor.
