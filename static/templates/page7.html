Quran - Surahs 61 to 70

61. As-Saff (The Ranks)

Meaning: Urges believers to fight for Allah’s cause with unity and sincerity.

62. Al-Jumu’ah (Friday)

Meaning: Importance of Friday prayer and reminder of neglectful behavior of past communities.

63. Al-Munafiqun (The Hypocrites)

Meaning: Exposes hypocrites’ traits and warns believers against their influence.

64. At-Taghabun (Mutual Disillusion)

Meaning: Day of Judgment will reveal true winners and losers, urging faith and charity.

65. At-Talaq (Divorce)

Meaning: Provides laws regarding divorce, waiting period, and reliance on Allah.

66. At-Tahrim (Prohibition)

Meaning: Addresses incidents involving the Prophet’s household and advises repentance.

67. Al-Mulk (The Sovereignty)

Meaning: Highlights Allah’s power in creation, and the consequences of disbelief.

68. Al-Qalam (The Pen)

Meaning: Defends the Prophet against accusations and gives parable of arrogant people.

69. Al-Haqqah (The Inevitable)

Meaning: Describes Day of Judgment, fate of previous nations, and people receiving records.

70. Al-Ma’arij (The Ascending Stairways)

Meaning: Describes disbelievers’ arrogance and the qualities of true believers who succeed.
