Quran - Surahs 1 to 10

             
 

1. Al-Fatiha (The Opening)

 

Meaning: Praise of Allah; worship, guidance, and mercy.

 
 

2. Al-Baqarah (The Cow)

 

Meaning: Guidance for believers; laws, stories of prophets, and lessons of faith.

 
 

3. Aal-E-Imran (The Family of Imran)

 

Meaning: Story of Imran’s family; faith, unity, and trust in Allah.

 
 

4. An-Nisa (The Women)

 

Meaning: Rights of women, justice, family laws, and social guidance.

 
 

5. Al-Maidah (The Table Spread)

 

Meaning: Laws about food, oaths, justice, and obedience to Allah.

 
 

6. Al-An’am (The Cattle)

 

Meaning: Oneness of Allah, rejection of shirk, and lessons from prophets.

 
 

7. Al-A’raf (The Heights)

 

Meaning: Stories of past prophets; heaven, hell, and divine warnings.

 
 

8. Al-Anfal (The Spoils of War)

 

Meaning: Rules of war, victory at Badr, and obedience to Allah and His Messenger.

 
 

9. At-Tawbah (The Repentance)

 

Meaning: Repentance, warning for hypocrites, and call to strive in Allah’s cause.

 
 

10. Yunus (Prophet Jonah)

 

Meaning: Story of Prophet Yunus; patience, guidance, and signs of Allah.
