Quran - Surahs 81 to 90

81. At-Takwir (The Overthrowing)

Meaning: Scenes of the end of the world and testimony of the Quran’s truth.

82. Al-Infitar (The Cleaving)

Meaning: Cosmic events on Judgment Day, angels recording deeds, and accountability.

83. Al-Mutaffifin (Defrauders)

Meaning: Condemnation of fraud in trade, rewards for the righteous, and punishment for sinners.

84. Al-Inshiqaq (The Splitting Asunder)

Meaning: The sky splitting, humans meeting their record, reward and punishment explained.

85. Al-Buruj (The Mansions of the Stars)

Meaning: Story of the persecuted believers, Allah’s power, and certainty of Judgment Day.

86. At-Tariq (The Morning Star)

Meaning: Oath by the heavens, creation of man, and Allah’s knowledge of all secrets.

87. Al-A’la (The Most High)

Meaning: Glorification of Allah, reminder of the Hereafter, and encouragement to purify the soul.

88. Al-Ghashiyah (The Overwhelming)

Meaning: Description of Hell and Paradise, urging reflection on creation and Allah’s signs.

89. Al-Fajr (The Dawn)

Meaning: Oath by dawn, destruction of ancient nations, fate of the arrogant, and reward for the righteous soul.

90. Al-Balad (The City)

Meaning: Life is struggle, freeing slaves, feeding poor, and choosing the path of righteousness.
